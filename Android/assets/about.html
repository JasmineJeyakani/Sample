About Us
Bayer is a global leader in providing innovative solutions to pest, weed and plant disease problems in the professional non-crop markets. This encompasses the turf and amenity sector, as well as professional and rural pest control, stored product protection, industrial vegetation management and horticulture.
Bayer's turf and amenity division offers a comprehensive and innovative range of products for greenkeepers, groundsmen and other professionals in the turf management industry. Its' vision is to "provide customers with first choice solutions to protect and care for the environment in which we all live, work and play".
